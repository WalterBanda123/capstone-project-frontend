Land Document Validation

Welcome to the Land Document Validation page. Here, you can validate the accuracy and legality of your land-related documents through our decentralized validation system.

Deed of Title
A deed of title, also known as a property deed, is a legal document that transfers ownership of a property from one party to another.
Survey Plan
A survey plan is a detailed drawing that outlines the boundaries, dimensions, and features of a property.
Property Tax Assessment
A property tax assessment is an official document issued by the local government that determines the value of a property for tax purposes.
Identification Document (e.g., ID Card, Passport, Marriage Certificate)
Identification documents such as ID cards, passports, or marriage certificates are used to verify the identity of individuals involved in land transactions.
